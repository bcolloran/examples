A RICH PICTURE OF CATEGORICAL DATA

Depicting the relationship between categorical variables can be tricky, and even moreso if your story needs to show the level of confidence you can have in those relationshipse. This visualization of the survey responses allow viewers to quickly see the "marginal distributions" of each individual variable, as well the relationships between the variables and the statistical confidence level in the strength of those relationships.

IMPACT:These plots played an important role in survey research conducted at Mozilla around key influencers of technology adoption. Before this research project, surveys had often been conducted in an ad-hoc manner, and the results were often viewed skeptically. By focusing on rigor and presenting reults in manner that visually conveyed the sophistication of the analysis, the research team was able to persuasively engage with the product team and successfully impact the product direction.
